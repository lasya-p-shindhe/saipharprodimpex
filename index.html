Our Product Range

Comprehensive solutions for moisture control and pharmaceutical packaging needs

Desiccants & Adsorbents

Premium moisture and odour control solutions for protecting your products from environmental damage.

  • Silica Gel (Orange, Blue, White indicating)
  • Molecular Sieves (3A, 4A, 5A, 13X)
  • Activated Carbon for odour control
  • Oxygen Absorbers
  • Custom desiccant canisters for filling
  • Various packaging sizes available

HDPE & PET Bottles

FDA approved pharmaceutical-grade bottles for safe storage and dispensing of medicines and supplements.

  • HDPE Bottles (High-Density Polyethylene)
  • PET Bottles (Polyethylene Terephthalate)
  • LDPE Bottles (Low-Density Polyethylene)
  • PP Bottles (Polypropylene)
  • Multiple sizes: 15ml to 1000ml
  • Amber and clear options available

Child Resistant & Tamper Evident Caps

Safety closures meeting international standards for pharmaceutical and nutraceutical packaging.

  • CRC (Child Resistant Closures)
  • CT (Tamper Evident Caps)
  • Euro Head Caps
  • Screw caps with liner seals
  • Multiple colors and sizes
  • Compatible with standard bottles

Oral Syringes

Precision dosing syringes for accurate liquid medication administration in healthcare settings.

  • 1ml to 100ml capacity range
  • Clear graduated markings
  • Medical grade plastic material
  • Oral syringe adaptors available
  • Sterile and non-sterile options
  • Bulk packaging available

Droppers & Measuring Solutions

Precision dispensing accessories for accurate liquid measurement and administration.

  • Dropper bottles (glass and plastic)
  • Euro Head caps with droppers
  • Bulb droppers in various sizes
  • Measuring cups with clear markings
  • Calibrated dosing spoons
  • Custom printing available

Complete Pharma Packaging

End-to-end pharmaceutical and medical packaging solutions tailored to your specific requirements.

  • Tablet and capsule bottles
  • Syrup and suspension bottles
  • Powder and granule containers
  • Desiccant integrated packaging
